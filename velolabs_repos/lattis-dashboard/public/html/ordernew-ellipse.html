The system will automatically create Ride IDs for this new batch of rides. These new rides can be found in the tab ‘Inactive Rides’ in your Ride Fleet.

You will receive a shipment from Velo Labs containing your new Ellipses and a set of stickers of each Bike ID. When you receive the order, place one sticker on each bike and attach an Ellipse. Then follow the instructions to activate each Ellipse.

As soon as an Ellipse and bike have been paired and activated, they are ready to be deployed. Once you’ve physically moved them to a hub, you can deploy them to the active fleet.
