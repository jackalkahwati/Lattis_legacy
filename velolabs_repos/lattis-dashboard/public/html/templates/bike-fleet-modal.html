Return Ride to 'Live' status

This action moves this Ride back into ‘Liveʼ status, making the Ride available to reserve. Only perform this action if the Ride has been physically deployed to a parking spot or zone where it can be reserved.

Send bike to workshop

This action temporarily moves the bike into ‘Suspended’ status - it will no longer appear available to riders in the Bikeshare app. Once you or your maintenance team has completed the work, and you’ve returned the bike to a rentable location, come back to the Fleet system and return it to the active fleet.

Permanently delete

This action permanently removes the bike from your fleet. Youʼll still be able to see all trip and transaction history related to this bike in the archive, but youʼll no longer be able to rent it out. This action cannot be undone.
